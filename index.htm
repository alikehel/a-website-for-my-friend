Life Way hits in 2018

Science and Fun, two flavors in our life. Each of them has a different character which specializes it, but they meet at end. They mix making a sense of our life. To conclude, we can say that when you LEARN while having fun and LAUGHing from the bottom of your deep heart during your life, you can LIVE a sustainable life and achieve your goals greatly. The power of those triple L (Learn, Laugh and Live) makes a sense of our life and gives it a meaning and that’s what we seek to achieve through our event.

Imagine that you’re in an event where science and fun are under one ceiling in a way that has never been before. In our event you can enjoy both beneficial scientific information and entertainment on one single day. In our event , boredom has no place where you can enjoy time having a scientific talk in an unusual way together with motivational talks given by professionals and having fun with great musical bands.
